# Customer Purchase Prediction in Tourism Industry

## Project Overview
This project aims to predict whether a customer is likely to purchase a product in the tourism industry. By analyzing customer attributes such as age, income, preferences, and lifestyle, businesses can make data-driven decisions to improve marketing efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## Business Impact
Predicting customer purchase decisions enables tourism companies to:
- **Identify high-potential customers** and focus marketing efforts on those most likely to convert.
- **Design personalized offers and promotions** that align with individual customer profiles.
- **Improve conversion rates** by targeting customers with relevant products at the right time.
- **Optimize marketing budgets** by reducing spending on low-probability leads.
- **Gain competitive advantage** by anticipating customer needs better than competitors.
- **Enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty** through tailored experiences and recommendations.

## Models Evaluated
| Model                          | Train Accuracy | Test Accuracy |
|--------------------------------|----------------|---------------|
| Logistic Regression            | 73.09%         | 74.75%       |
| KNN Classifier                 | 91.68%         | 87.09%       |
| Support Vector Classifier (SVC)| 81.57%         | 79.41%       |
| Naive Bayes (BernoulliNB)      | 68.19%         | 68.77%       |
| XGBoost (XGBClassifier)        | 90.08%         | 87.34%       |

## Conclusion
The **XGBoost (XGBClassifier)** model performed the best with a **test accuracy of 87.34%**. It offers robust predictions for identifying potential buyers, helping tourism companies optimize marketing strategies, increase conversions, and improve customer satisfaction.
